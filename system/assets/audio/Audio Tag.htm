No audio player script being used at all!

This is just relying on the browser's native MP3 support. No heavy Javascript that calls the mother ship every 10ms, no expensive licensing, and it works offline. In a non-HTML5-compliant browser like IE8, the audio controls won't appear at all.

So when can we use this? When WinXP and IE8 are effectively dead (all support came to an end on April 8, 2014).
